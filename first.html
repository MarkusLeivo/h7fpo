High Spirits



You can try meditation:


  • Find yourself a comfortable place to sit
  • Close your eyes (or leave them slightly open if you prefer)
  • Take a few deep breaths
  • Take a few moments to loosen your body from ypur head to your toes
  • Take a few more slow breaths
  • Stop to notice the sensations throughout your body — the warmth, the coolness or any discomfort.
    Be aware of them, but don't let them concern you too much.
  • Pick one sensation — such as the feeling of your breath going in and out — and devote your attention to it.
    Just focus on that.
  • When your mind wanders, bring your attention back to the breath. After a few moments, your mind may wander again.
    Once again, notice that and simply return your attention back to the present moment.
  • When you're ready — after one minute, 10 minutes or 30 minutes — get your attention back to the present, open your eyes.
